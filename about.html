About the Firm

L S D & CO a firm of Chartered Accountants having experience of more than a decade. We believe in “MUTUAL GROWTH" and are well equipped & resourceful to provide single window solution for all Financial & Accounting Management, Tax & Business Advisory, Secretarial & Compliances, Corporate Law Matters, Debt & Equity, Corporate & Legal matters..

The firm was established in the year October 2012 and now having four partners. The firm offers a wide range of specialized and multi-disciplinary professional services which meets all the needs of the business. The Partners of L S D & CO have varied experience and blend of knowledge and youthful energy.



To help our clients we explore all the issues in depth to get a clear picture of their goals and objectives and help them in weighing the impact of various decisions. We ensure that our clients work is performed to the highest quality standards and to the complete satisfaction of the clients.
