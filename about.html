About Us

About the Firm

L S D & CO a firm emerging fast in the field of chartered services, is a result of synergy of its partners & mentors having wide cumulative experience of more than 30 years. We believe in "GROWING WITH OTHERS" and are well equipped to provide all the chartered & related services under one roof. L S D & CO possesses best blend of knowledge and experience which can provide one window solution for all Accounting, Compliances, Taxation, Finance & Legal matters. It is having penetrating experience in the areas of Statutory Audit, Internal Audit, Compliance Audit, Process Review, Company Law Matters, Taxations (Direct & Indirect) including International Taxation, Appeals and Management Consultancy and Finance. The company provides external audit services through its associate accounting firm. We also have networking with professional firms in other cities enabling us to provide services in other areas of the country.

L S D & CO operate in complete computerized environment and is looking for substantial expansion in near future. The firm has a modern working setup in terms of well furnished spacious offices, supported by latest technologies. We act ethically and are committed to provide the highest quality services to our clients so as to meet and exceed their expectations. To help our clients we explore all the issues in depth to get a clear picture of their goals and objectives, and help them in weighing the impact of various decisions. We ensure that our clients work is performed to the highest quality standards, within budget and timelines.

Mission:

  • Outstanding values to the client through value added, quality, timely and cost effective services.

  • Create a reputable brand name in market and through associations in international markets.

  • To help the accountants in developing their careers in accounting/auditing and exposing them to the industry in the field with practical experience and professional guidance.
