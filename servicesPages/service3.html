Taxation


Successful companies consider tax implications before they make business decisions. This makes tax compliance and tax advice a crucial element in their business process.

Singhi & Co is well positioned to offer individuals and companies comprehensive tax services. Our tax professionals are selected for their commitment to the profession as well as their tax planning expertise.

Key areas of tax services:


  • Tax Audit - required under the statute in India
  • Tax Compliance - Computation of tax and filing the returns with the authorities
  • Tax Advisory - Advise on specific Tax matters
  • International Tax - Advise on international tax issues under various treaties
  • Transfer Pricing - Advisory and audits relating to transfer pricing
  • Individual taxation -
» Expatriate/ Foreign National taxation

» For Indian individuals – Generally these services are provided to a collective set of individuals for example all employees of a company

  • Indirect Taxation - Advise and compliance on various indirect taxes such as VAT, Sales Tax, Excise, Service tax etc.  
  • Representation in Tax investigation/ litigation/ departmental reviews
