Corporate Finance



Corporate finance is the area of finance dealing with the sources of funding and the capital structure of corporations, the actions that managers take to increase the value of the firm to the shareholders, and the tools and analysis used to allocate financial resources. L S D & CO helps you at every step to enhance the value of business and transactions and thus creating value for you.
