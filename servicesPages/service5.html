Company formation & law



We are a leader in emerging name in the field of offering Company Law Consultancy Services. With Companies functioning in India governed by Companies Act, 1956, each company needs to get registered with Registrar of Companies (ROC) and file necessary documents for different statutory requirements.

 Our services include:

  • Formation of new company.
  • Annual Compliance under the Companies Act,1956
  • Change of name, object clause
  • Authorized capital, directorship, registered office
  • Drafting of Minutes, Notice, Resolutions
  • Buy Back of Shares
  • Issue and Allotment of shares
  • Winding up of Companies
  • Filing of various petitions with Company Law Board
  • Procurement of various approvals under the Companies Act,1956
